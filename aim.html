The main purpose of the Linear Algebra Vector Checker

Aim

The aim of this virtual lab is to help learners understand linear dependence and linear independence through direct practice.

Users can enter vectors, analyse them with mathematical methods, and quickly connect each result to the idea of span, rank, and basis.

The tool supports spaces from ℝ² to ℝ¹⁰, with visual interpretation in 2D and 3D and full algebraic analysis in every supported dimension.
