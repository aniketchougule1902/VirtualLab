Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take to get a response to my feedback?

A: We strive to respond to all feedback within 2-3 business days. Bug reports and critical issues are addressed as quickly as possible.

Q: Can I use this tool in my classroom or online course?

A: Yes! The Linear Algebra Vector Checker is free for educational use. We'd love to hear how you're using it in your teaching.

Q: Do you offer custom versions for specific educational institutions?

A: We're open to collaborations with educational institutions. Please contact us at partnerships@mathvortexlabs.com to discuss possibilities.

Q: How can I contribute to the development of this tool?

A: Check our GitHub repository for contribution guidelines. We welcome code contributions, documentation improvements, and testing assistance.

Q: Is my personal information safe when I submit feedback?

A: Yes, we take privacy seriously. We only use your information to respond to your feedback and improve our tools. We never share your information with third parties without your consent.

Report a Bug

How to Report Bugs Effectively

To help us fix issues quickly, please include as much detail as possible:

  1. Description: What exactly went wrong?
  2. Steps to Reproduce: Step-by-step instructions to recreate the issue
  3. Expected Behavior: What should have happened?
  4. Actual Behavior: What actually happened?
  5. Environment: Browser, operating system, device type
  6. Screenshots: If possible, include screenshots or screen recordings

You can report bugs through the contact form or directly on GitHub.
