About the Team

The Linear Algebra Vector Checker was created by first-year B.Tech I.T. students from Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering (PCCOE), Pune. The project combines interface design, mathematical content, simulation thinking, and documentation into one learning tool.

Built for Learning

The team focused on making linear algebra easier to explore through simple inputs and readable outputs.

Designed for Clarity

Each page was shaped to help students move from theory to testing without confusion.

Developed Together

The work was shared across development, theory writing, research, and content review.

What the Project Focuses On

Clear Theory

Easy explanations for span, linear combination, basis, rank, and dependence.

Interactive Practice

Students can test vectors directly and compare their own reasoning with computed results.

Continuous Improvement

The team keeps refining content, design, and usability based on classroom-style learning needs.
